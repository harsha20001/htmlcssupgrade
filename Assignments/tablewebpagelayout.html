About Hotel :

Hotels are establishments that provide lodging and accommodation services to travelers and guests. They come in a wide range of types and sizes, from budget-friendly motels to luxurious five-star resorts. Typically, hotels offer various amenities and services, such as guest rooms, restaurants, bars, meeting and event spaces, fitness centers, and concierge services.

One of the key factors that differentiate hotels is their star rating system. This system, often ranging from one to five stars, helps guests gauge the level of luxury, comfort, and service they can expect. Higher-starred hotels tend to offer more spacious and well-appointed rooms, better dining options, and additional facilities like spas and pools.

In recent years, the hotel industry has also been impacted by changing consumer preferences, with a growing interest in sustainable and eco-friendly practices. Many hotels are now implementing green initiatives, such as energy-efficient lighting, water conservation measures, and waste reduction programs, to align with the increasing environmental consciousness of their guests.
